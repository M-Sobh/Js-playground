ABC University

ABC University, with its nearly 110-year tradition is one of the oldest and the largest universities in the country. It is also one of the most modern universities, where tradition and modernity meet. The campus has a historic part, with an 19th. century palace, and a contemporary part where all faculty buildings and dormitories are situated.

ABC University offers good facilities and infrastructure in one place. On our 120-hectare main campus we have 17 dormitories, a modern library, a sports centre (with tennis courts, a sports hall and a swimming pool) a language centre, a veterinary clinic and much more. The campus is located in the southern part of the city and can be easily reached from the city centre by subway or by bus. Over 1,250 university teachers and instructors make sure that you will gain plenty of knowledge and practical skills. At present, the university consists of 3 faculties; there are 8,000 students enrolled.

ABC University has been declared number one in the rankings of social science universities, and has always been in the top of country universities.

ABC University was acknowledged as “The most innovative and creative university at creating job perspectives”.

Study programmes

The range of the academic programmes is systematically enlarged and currently includes 13 study programmes. Within study programmes there are 45 areas of specializations.

Academic programmes are offered at the bachelor, master and doctoral levels, there is also a postgraduate MBA programme.

International Cooperation

International collaboration plays an important role in University activities by contributing to the expansion of research and standardization of teaching programmes. Overall, the University collaborates with 19 international partners from all continents. On the annual basis, about 3500 University employees and students travel internationally (including about 340 students participating in international practical training programmes).

ABC University is a member of prestigious international organizations such as the FRTY, European Universities Association (EUA), BBICZ and many others. The University is active among regional institutions by offering modern education programmes and highly qualified experts. Since 1990 ABC University has actively participated in the DFRE programmes as a university from an associate, a candidate and since 2004 from an SSF Member state. Currently, the staff of ABC University participate in European educational programmes (Erasmus, Erasmus Mundus, CEEPUS, Leonardo da Vinci, Grundtvig, Jean Monnet, Tempus) and research programmes (6 and 7 Framework Programme, EUREKA, COST, Culture).

For international students, we also offer seven master programs (of duration of three or four semesters) and 11-semester programme Veterinary Medicine leading to a lekarz weterynarii (equiv. to brit. Veterinary Surgeon) degree, all taught exclusively in English.
